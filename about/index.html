Our team

Tiffany Nguyen
President
Hello! I am here at Design at UCI for (most likely) the same reasons you are reading this and checking out this organization: to be a part of a design community at UCIrvine and grow as a designer. My interests span from technology to design and even to psychology. With these interests in mind, I decided to study Informatics and specialize in Human-Computer Interaction. I got my early start designing spreads for my high school yearbook, but little did I know how design can be a means of problem-solving and not just about making things visually appealing (well, that’s another big part of it). After digging around and learning more about the different design disciplines, I have found an interest in the world of UI/UX and product design. While I would have loved to have a design-oriented organization and community on campus during my first few years of college, there wasn’t. Hopefully, Design at UCI will be that community for you and all who are interested in design, and I am excited to be a part of a team dedicated to creating exactly that.
Outside of design, here are a few facts about me: Spotify is my best friend. My closet is slowly becoming a collection of mostly thrifted clothes and I am all for that. Indie/alternative is my go-to musical genre. I am a hoard—when it comes to bookmarking and saving Medium articles (mostly design-related). I think film scores are underrated. I hope to one day create more content than I consume. I don’t have a spirit pokemon because pokemon never really made it into my childhood. Creative Type According to Adobe, my creative type is the “Dreamer”, whose creative strengths include “connections to emotions and imagination, empathy and sensitivity”—which I definitely all resonate with. I’m a daydreamer at heart and I lean more on the idealistic side. I hope to let my imagination and empathy be driving forces in how I design.
