UI-UX Product Design Speaker Panel

August 27 5:30 PM

Hosted by Engineered Careers

Exclusive Design at UCI members-only event

Featuring

Nerissa Rosario

Lily Konings

Mandy Han

Grace Park

Learn more about UI-UX Product Design from industry professionals at top companies. The speakers will talk about their experience within UI-UX and Product Design, coming from Facebook, Twitch, Blizzard, WalletJoy and CloudSpot.

Meet the Speakers

Nerissa Rosario
Say hi to Nerissa! Graduating from UCI as an Informatics major, she is now a Product Designer for WalletJoy. Outside of work, Nerissa loves to spend time with her white maltipoo but unfortunately cannot travel as much as she’d like (social distancing!). She takes pride in staying organized both in real life and online!
Lily Konings
Meet Lily! She’s a Product Designer at Instagram who’s responsible for making sure your messaging experience is seamless! She’s even worked at AngelList where she improved the dreaded job search experience for millions of people. Whether it be tennis or Settlers of Catan, she’s confident she can beat you in both!
Mandy Han
Mandy’s your friendly and creative handy woman! She’s a San Francisco native with a background in illustration and storytelling. Aside from being a Product Designer at Twitch, she loves to create films, explore new foods, and watch the latest trending Youtube videos!
Grace Park
Grace is a recent Bootcamp graduate getting her feet wet in the world of UX/UI. She thrives in a teamwork-focused environment where mutual understanding and respect is shared. In her spare time, she enjoys doodling on her iPad and cherishing memories through making YouTube videos. Make sure to smash that subscribe button!
Chris Liao
Chris' strong passion for both great design and great games paved the road for his career at Blizzard. Although he started in web and mobile development, he has since moved on to cheating games. In his spare time, you'll find him playing them...for research of course.
